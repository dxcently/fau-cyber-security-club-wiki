cyber careers.

One of the best DevOps creators with beginner‑friendly and visual explanations

https://www.youtube.com/@TechWorldwithNana

technical foundation.

https://www.youtube.com/@LowLevelTV

and real-world cyber concepts.

Excellent for beginners and intermediate learners.

https://www.youtube.com/@_JohnHammond

Specializes in reverse engineering and malware analysis. Great for blue‑teamers

https://www.youtube.com/@EricParker
